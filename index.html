Introduction

I am a fourth-year Doctor student majoring in Control Theory and Control Engineering, advised by Prof. En Li and Prof. Zize Liang. Before that, I received my B.S. degree in College of Electrical Engineering from Sichuan University in 2019.

My research interest includes computer vision and robot control, especially in object detection manipulator control. My work covers pose estimation, establishment of robot system, and weakly supervised object detection.
